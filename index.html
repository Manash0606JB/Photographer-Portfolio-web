Hello,

my name is Dhritisman

I'm a freelance photographer from the state of Assam, India. I presented my unique vision of the nature and its products to you people by capturing them with my phone camera. It all ultimately comes down to showing respect to nature and understanding the things and beautiful phenomenons around you.

Dhritisman Barman is an Assam based freelance photographer specializing in both portraits and landscapes. He was born on 13th of December, 2005. He is in Nalbari, Assam from the day he was born. His major field of interests are nature and architectures in landcapes while flora in portraits. In a place like Assam where both urban and rural beauties can be sight, he started to inspire and capture those beauties.

He is a college student now exploring his passion and learning more about photography. He is a self-taught photographer and he started to show some interest in photography when he got his first personal smartphone, Vivo Y91. Yes, you read it right! He is a mobile photographer. All the photographs in the site's gallery section or in his social media platforms are all captured by a low-end smartphone. He take a big step towards his passion of photography when he was 16 years old. And now after he is 18, he wants to take mobile photography to a next level.

As a learner in photography, Dhritisman has won the prestigious SkyLover Award of the Year 2022 and also he was nominated for Pofesonál Phophogafár Award.His few works were also published in famous magazines like PhotoGraphié and Aperture.

Now he is exploring different places for his photography. With the believe that there are no limits or barriers for photography, from a cheap low-end smartphone user to an expensive mirrorless camera user, anyone can be good photographer. Its just depends on their field of view, what they actually wants to capture?

God creates the beauty. My camera and I are a witness.
